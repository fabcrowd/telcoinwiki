How Telcoin’s pieces fit together

Use this primer to understand the relationship between the Telcoin Association, Telcoin Network, TEL token, and TELx liquidity layer. Each card links to the official resource for in-depth exploration.

Systems at a glance

Association

Governance anchor

The Telcoin Association is the Swiss Verein that oversees validator onboarding, issuance policies, and community councils.

Read the Association overview →

Network

Carrier-backed Layer 1

EVM-compatible blockchain validated by GSMA-member MNOs, powering remittances, Digital Cash, and TELx execution.

Explore the network →

TEL

Incentive & utility token

TEL fuels transactions, staking programs, and governance participation, aligning validators and liquidity providers.

Review TEL documentation →

TELx

Liquidity coordination

TELx connects TEL, Digital Cash, and user incentives to power swaps, remittances, and DeFi integrations.

Dive into TELx →
