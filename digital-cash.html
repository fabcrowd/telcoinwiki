      Digital Cash

      Instant-settlement fiat on the Telcoin Network

      Digital Cash brings fiat-backed currencies like eUSD, eCAD, and ePHP directly into the Telcoin Wallet. Each asset is designed to settle in seconds on the Telcoin Network while preserving the compliance expectations of its underlying fiat.

      Currency lineup

      eUSD

      USD-denominated Digital Cash for remittances, swaps, and TELx liquidity. Backed by regulated custodians.

      How Digital Cash is used

      Remittances

      Senders can hold eUSD and convert to local fiat at the last mile, reducing FX exposure while keeping transparent fees.

      Merchant payouts
